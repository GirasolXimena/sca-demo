The Queer Discount

What is the queer discount?

I offer queer clientele discounted prices on both tattoos and merchandise.

How does it work?

Members of the queer community are charged a discounted hourly rate for one-session pieces, or a discounted flat session fee for multi-session projects.

Additionally, you can enter code "queerdiscount" for 10% off any item in my online shop and etsy store
